Entrepreneurship is the pursuit of establishing a new venture. Having an idea or concept of creating a product, service or process is the main reason why one becomes an entrepreneur. This involves studying the enterprise or project in order to plan, prepare and launch the venture. Creativity and innovation can be incorporated into its development: creativity is the formulation of original ideas that is considered new, and innovation is the process of converting an idea into a product, service or process that creates value for others.
Project F.J.H. is a sole proprietorship founded by Faisal Javed Hussain that operates a chain of ventures. The ventures comprise of enterprises and projects: the former are business ventures and the latter are social ventures.
The first venture is Transition, an enterprise that provides design services in the form of the three states of matter: solid, liquid and gas. Solid is the formation of a digital design, liquid is the creation of a cash flow, and gas is the production of marketing materials. In addition, plasma is presented as the fourth state of matter, as an extra service area that creates further designs.
The second venture is Fusion, an enterprise that fuses photos into the design of a music CD. Using the design of a music album, sets of photos are assembled into a titled list to create a photo album. Using the design of a music single, a set of photos is assembled and given a title to create a photo single. The four elements: earth, water, air and fire determine how the album or single is designed. In addition, aether is presented as the fifth element, as an extra service area that personalises the design.
